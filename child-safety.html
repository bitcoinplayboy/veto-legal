VETO

Child Safety Standards

Effective 15 June 2026

VETO ("VETO", "we", "us") operates a private, verified matchmaking and curated networking service for verified adults worldwide. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM). This document sets out the standards we maintain to prevent, detect, and act on any such conduct, in line with Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy and applicable law.

VETO is strictly an adults-only (18+) service. Minors are not permitted to create accounts or use the app under any circumstances. Every member must pass a live identity and age verification before gaining access.

1. Prohibited conduct

The following are strictly forbidden on VETO and result in immediate removal, a permanent ban, and reporting to the relevant authorities:

  • Any child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — images, video, text, or links — in profiles, messages, or any other surface.
  • Sexualisation of minors, or any content that exploits or endangers a child.
  • Grooming, solicitation, enticement, or any attempt to contact, meet, or obtain content from a minor.
  • Use of the service by anyone under 18, or facilitating access for a minor.
  • Trafficking, sextortion, or any other form of child exploitation.

2. How we prevent and detect CSAE

  • Age gate: registration is blocked for anyone under 18, and date of birth is collected and enforced at sign-up.
  • Identity & liveness verification: members complete a live selfie matched to a government identity record, which confirms the person is a real adult. Female members are verified before they appear; the directory is closed to unverified accounts.
  • Moderation: profiles, photos, and reports are reviewed, and accounts that violate these standards are removed.
  • Reporting & blocking: every member can block any user and report a user or a specific message from within the app at any time.

3. Reporting child safety concerns

If you encounter content or behaviour that may involve a child, report it immediately:

  • In the app: use the Report action on the offending profile or message.
  • By email: alpha.model.dev@gmail.com — our designated child-safety point of contact. We aim to acknowledge reports within 24 hours.

We review every report promptly. Confirmed violations lead to removal of the content, a permanent ban of the account, preservation of relevant evidence, and reporting to the appropriate authorities.

4. Reporting to authorities

We report apparent child sexual abuse material and exploitation to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and the appropriate national law-enforcement and child-protection agencies in the jurisdictions where our members are located, and we cooperate with lawful investigations.

5. Legal compliance

We comply with applicable child-safety laws in the jurisdictions where our members are located. These standards are maintained alongside our Privacy Policy and form part of our commitment to a safe community.

6. Point of contact

Child-safety matters, including requests from Google or law enforcement: alpha.model.dev@gmail.com.
